Vacuum packaging removes air to prevent oxidation and bacterial growth, offering extended freshness. Other packaging methods include traditional techniques like skin packaging and tray sealing, focusing on convenience and extended usability. Each method aims to preserve freshness, ensure food safety, and meet consumer demand for high-quality products.</p></p>
