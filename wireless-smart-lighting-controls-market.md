<p><ul><li>Zigbee</li><li>Wifi</li><li>Bluetooth</li><li>Z-Wave</li><li>Enocean</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless smart lighting controls market comprises several key technologies: Zigbee, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, Z-Wave, and EnOcean. Zigbee is known for its low power consumption and mesh networking capabilities, making it ideal for large installations. Wi-Fi offers high bandwidth and convenience, leveraging existing networks. Bluetooth is user-friendly and suitable for short-range applications, while Z-Wave operates on low frequencies, ensuring reliable communication in smart homes. EnOcean emphasizes energy harvesting, employing self-powered devices for sustainable lighting solutions. Together, these technologies enhance smart lighting efficiency and accessibility.</p></p>
