<p><p>The garbage truck market is categorized into three main types: front loaders, rear loaders, and side loaders. Front loaders are designed for efficient bulk waste collection, utilizing a hydraulic mechanism to lift dumpsters. Rear loaders, the most common type, have a compaction system at the back, allowing for manual or automated loading. Side loaders feature a robotic arm for collecting waste from the side, facilitating easy access to containers. Each type serves distinct needs in waste management and collection efficiency.</p></p>
