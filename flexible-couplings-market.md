<p><ul><li>Double Thread</li><li>Parallel Lines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Flexible Couplings market encompasses two main types: Double Thread and Parallel Lines. Double Thread couplings provide enhanced torque transmission and accommodate misalignment through a twisted design, making them ideal for high-vibration environments. In contrast, Parallel Lines couplings focus on maintaining alignment while absorbing shock and reducing noise, often used in machinery with consistent rotational speeds. Both types serve crucial roles in various industries by improving system performance, ensuring durability, and minimizing wear and tear on connected components.</p></p>
