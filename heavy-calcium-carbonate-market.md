<p><ul><li>Dry Process</li><li>Wet Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heavy calcium carbonate market is primarily segmented into two production processes: dry and wet. The dry process involves grinding limestone into a fine powder without water, leading to high purity and lower production costs. In contrast, the wet process utilizes water to produce a finer, purer product, often resulting in a more uniform particle size and better surface area. Each method caters to different applications in industries such as plastics, paints, and food, influencing market preferences and pricing strategies.</p></p>
