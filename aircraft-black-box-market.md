<p><p>The aircraft black box market encompasses two primary types: Flight Data Recorders (FDRs) and Cockpit Voice Recorders (CVRs). FDRs capture crucial flight parameters, such as altitude, speed, and engine performance, enabling thorough investigation of accidents. CVRs record cockpit conversations and ambient sounds, providing insights into crew actions and decision-making during flights. Both devices are essential for enhancing aviation safety, facilitating post-incident analyses, and complying with regulatory requirements, thereby driving demand in the aerospace sector.</p></p>
