<p><p>The Dragging Equipment Detector market encompasses devices designed to identify and monitor equipment dragging along a surface, enhancing safety in various industries. Stationary impact plates are fixed sensors that detect disturbances caused by dragging equipment, providing immediate alerts. Traditional paddle sets, on the other hand, consist of movable paddles that trigger alarms when impacted by dragging equipment, offering a simpler, mechanical detection method. Both types are critical for preventing accidents and ensuring operational efficiency in heavy machinery environments.</p></p>
