<p><p>The solid nitrogen fertilizer market comprises several key types. Urea is the most widely used, known for its high nitrogen content and versatility. Ammonium nitrate provides quick nitrogen availability and improves crop yields. Ammonium sulfate contributes both nitrogen and sulfur, enhancing soil quality. Calcium ammonium nitrate (CAN) combines calcium and nitrogen for balanced nutrition and better soil structure. Other market options include fertilizers with varied formulations catering to specific crop needs or soil conditions, reinforcing nitrogen's role in agriculture.</p></p>
