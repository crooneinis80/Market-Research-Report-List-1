<p><strong>The SMD Oven Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Convection Oven</li><li>Vapour Phase Oven</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SMD oven market includes two primary types: convection ovens and vapor phase ovens. Convection ovens utilize forced hot air circulation to ensure even heating and efficient soldering of surface-mounted devices (SMDs), making them popular for a wide range of applications. In contrast, vapor phase ovens rely on the boiling point of a specially engineered fluid to heat components, offering precise temperature control and minimizing thermal shock, making them ideal for sensitive electronics. Both types cater to the growing demand for high-quality soldering processes.</p></p>
