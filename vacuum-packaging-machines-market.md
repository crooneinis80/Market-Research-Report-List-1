<p><p>The vacuum packaging machines market is segmented into three main types: Thermoformers, External Vacuum Sealers, and Others. Thermoformers mold plastic sheets into specific shapes, effectively sealing products for extended freshness. External Vacuum Sealers remove air from bags before sealing, making them popular for home use and small-scale applications. The "Others" category encompasses diverse machines like chamber vacuum sealers and inline systems, catering to varied commercial needs. Each type addresses unique packaging requirements across food, pharmaceuticals, and other industries.</p></p>
