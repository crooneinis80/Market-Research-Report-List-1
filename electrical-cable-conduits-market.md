<p><p>The electrical cable conduits market consists of flexible and rigid conduits, each serving distinct purposes. Flexible conduits are made from materials like PVC or metal, allowing for easy installation in tight spaces and offering durability against vibration and movement. In contrast, rigid conduits are typically constructed from materials such as PVC or steel and provide enhanced protection against environmental hazards, making them suitable for outdoor or high-stress applications. Both types are crucial for safeguarding electrical cables and ensuring efficient wiring systems.</p></p>
