<p><p>NdFeB (Neodymium Iron Boron) permanent magnets are widely used due to their high magnetic strength. The market is divided into two main types: Bonded NdFeB and Sintered NdFeB. Bonded NdFeB magnets are made by mixing magnetic powder with a binder, offering flexibility in shapes and cost-effectiveness for smaller applications. Sintered NdFeB magnets are produced through high-temperature processing, delivering superior magnetic performance and durability, ideal for high-performance applications like motors and electronics. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, balancing cost and performance.</p></p>
