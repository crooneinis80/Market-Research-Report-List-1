<p><p>The PE compounds market includes HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) Type, XLPE (Cross-Linked Polyethylene) Type, and Other types. HDPE compounds are known for their high strength, durability, and chemical resistance, making them ideal for pipes, packaging, and industrial applications. XLPE compounds, with their cross-linked molecular structure, offer superior thermal and mechanical properties, commonly used in cables and insulation.
