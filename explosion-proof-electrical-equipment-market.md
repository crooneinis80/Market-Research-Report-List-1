<p><p>The explosion-proof electrical equipment market comprises various types designed to operate safely in hazardous environments. The flame-proof type contains explosions within a protective enclosure. Increased safety type enhances protection by reducing ignition risks. Intrinsic safety type prevents electrical sparks through design limits. Positive-pressure type maintains a safe atmosphere by using pressurized gas. Oil-immersed type utilizes non-flammable oil for insulation, while sand-filled type encases components in sand to absorb shocks. Other types are tailored for specific applications or environments.</p></p>
