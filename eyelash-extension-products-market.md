<p><p>The eyelash extension products market can be categorized into three main types: vitamin-based, prostaglandin-derived, and peptide-based formulations. Vitamin-based products typically enhance lash health and growth through nourishing ingredients like biotin and panthenol. Prostaglandin-derived products stimulate lash growth by mimicking natural hormones, often leading to longer, fuller lashes. Peptide-based solutions utilize specific amino acids to promote lash strength and resilience, improving overall appearance. Each type addresses unique consumer needs, contributing to a diverse and growing market.</p></p>
