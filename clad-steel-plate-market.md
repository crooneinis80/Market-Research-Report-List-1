<p><strong>The Clad Steel Plate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Explosion Bonded</li><li>Roll Bonded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Clad steel plates combine two or more metal layers, offering enhanced properties like corrosion resistance and strength. Explosion-bonded clad plates are created using controlled explosions to fuse dissimilar metals, ensuring a strong, durable bond ideal for demanding applications like chemical processing. Roll-bonded clad plates are produced by hot rolling layers together, resulting in a uniform, cost-effective product suitable for industries like construction and shipbuilding. Both methods cater to diverse industrial needs, balancing performance and economic efficiency.</p></p>
