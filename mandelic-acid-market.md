<p><p>Mandelic acid, a versatile alpha hydroxy acid (AHA), is available in three market types based on its molecular structure: DL-Type, L-Type, and D-Type. DL-Type is a racemic mixture of both L and D enantiomers, commonly used in skincare for exfoliation and acne treatment. L-Type, the levorotatory form, is often preferred in pharmaceuticals and cosmetics for its enhanced biological activity. D-Type, the dextrorotatory form, is less common but used in specialized applications. Each type offers unique properties tailored to specific industrial or cosmetic needs.</p></p>
