<p><p>The wall hanging furnace market is categorized into non-condensing and condensing types. Non-condensing furnaces operate at lower efficiency levels, releasing exhaust gases at higher temperatures, which can lead to energy loss. In contrast, condensing furnaces capture and reuse heat from exhaust gases, achieving higher efficiency rates and lower emissions. As energy efficiency becomes a priority, the demand for condensing types is rising, while non-condensing types may appeal to cost-conscious consumers or specific applications.</p></p>
