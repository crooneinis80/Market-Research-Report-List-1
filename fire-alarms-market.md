<p><ul><li>Addressable Systems</li><li>Conventional</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire alarms market primarily consists of two main types: addressable systems and conventional systems. Addressable systems utilize a digital address for each device, allowing for precise identification and quicker response to incidents. They are ideal for larger, more complex installations. In contrast, conventional systems group devices into zones, making them simpler and often more cost-effective for smaller applications. While addressable systems offer advanced features, conventional systems remain popular due to their ease of use and lower upfront costs.</p></p>
