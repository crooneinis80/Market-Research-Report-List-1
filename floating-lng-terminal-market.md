<p><p>The Floating LNG Terminal market consists of two main types: Floating LNG Import Terminals and Floating LNG Export Terminals. Floating LNG Import Terminals facilitate the reception, storage, and regasification of LNG, enabling countries to import natural gas without the need for extensive onshore infrastructure. Conversely, Floating LNG Export Terminals focus on the liquefaction and storage of natural gas, allowing producers to export LNG efficiently. Both types enhance flexibility and reduce the capital costs associated with traditional land-based facilities.</p></p>
