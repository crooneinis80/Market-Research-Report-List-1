<p><ul><li>Diaphragm Valves</li><li>Bellows Valves</li><li>Vacuum Valves</li><li>Ball Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor valve market includes various types crucial for controlling fluid and gas flow in manufacturing processes. Diaphragm valves utilize a flexible membrane to provide leak-proof sealing, while bellows valves use a series of folds for tight shutoff and vacuum integrity. Vacuum valves are designed to maintain low-pressure environments essential in semiconductor fabrication. Ball valves offer quick on/off control with a spherical disk, ensuring minimal flow resistance. Together, these valves support precision and reliability in sensitive semiconductor operations.</p></p>
