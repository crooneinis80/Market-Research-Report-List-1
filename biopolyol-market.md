<p><ul><li>Polyether Polyol</li><li>Polyester Polyol</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biopolyol market is primarily divided into two types: polyether polyol and polyester polyol. Polyether polyols are synthesized from renewable resources and are known for their flexibility and durability, making them ideal for applications in flexible foams and coatings. Polyester polyols, on the other hand, are derived from the reaction of diols and dicarboxylic acids, offering superior chemical resistance and thermal stability. Both types serve as sustainable alternatives in the production of various polyurethane products, reducing environmental impact.</p></p>
