<p><p>The Frequency Response Analyzer market comprises three key waveform types: sine wave, square wave, and triangle wave. Sine wave analysis is crucial for assessing linear systems' behavior in response to smooth, continuous signals. Square wave testing is used to evaluate systems' transient response and bandwidth, revealing nonlinearities and distortions. Triangle wave analysis helps in understanding system behavior under ramping conditions, beneficial for assessing frequency response and stability. Each waveform serves specific applications, driving demand across various industries, including electronics and telecommunications.</p></p>
