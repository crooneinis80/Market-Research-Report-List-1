<p><ul><li>Temporary</li><li>Semi-permanent</li><li>Permanent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hair colorants market can be categorized into three main types: temporary, semi-permanent, and permanent. Temporary colorants coat the hair shaft, lasting through a few washes, ideal for short-term changes. Semi-permanent dyes penetrate the hair slightly, providing vibrant shades that fade gradually over several weeks. Permanent colorants, on the other hand, chemically alter the hair structure, offering long-lasting color that endures multiple washes, making it suitable for those seeking a more enduring transformation. Each type caters to different consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
