<p><p>Solar Encapsulant Film is a critical material used in photovoltaic (PV) modules to protect solar cells from environmental factors such as moisture, dust, and mechanical stress. It acts as an adhesive layer, bonding the solar cells to the glass and backsheet, ensuring durability and efficiency of the solar panel. Common materials used include ethylene-vinyl acetate (EVA), polyolefin elastomers (POE), and polyvinyl butyral (PVB).</p><p>The Solar Encapsulant Film Market is experiencing steady growth, driven by the increasing adoption of solar energy globally. The market is expected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 6.2% during the forecast period. There is also a growing preference for POE-based encapsulants due to their superior performance in harsh environments. Furthermore, the increasing focus on sustainability is driving the adoption of recyclable and eco-friendly encapsulant films. These trends are expected to shape the future of the Solar Encapsulant Film Market, supporting its growth trajectory.</p></p>
