<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Slitter</li><li>CNC Slitter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic slitter market is primarily categorized into Hydraulic Slitters and CNC Slitters. Hydraulic Slitters use hydraulic power for precise cutting, offering high efficiency and the ability to handle thicker materials. They are ideal for mass production and heavy-duty applications. Conversely, CNC Slitters utilize computer numerical control for enhanced precision and automation, making them suitable for complex cuts and smaller batch sizes. Both types cater to diverse industries, including packaging, textiles, and metal processing, driving demand for advanced cutting solutions.</p></p>
